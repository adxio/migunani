Our Company

CV Migunani & Mberkahi, hereafter the ‘Migunani’, is a Yogyakarta based knowledge organization focusing on knowledge works such as research and evaluation. The Migunani will provide an unparalleled team of evaluators with a track record of producing high impact evaluation/research outputs.

Migunani, as a knowledge-focused organisation, engages in development through the use of the organisation’s key resource, its technical staff. The work is primarily about building human capital through and with people in participatory ways, for better knowledge-based development outcomes for families and communities. The primary mission of Migunani is to promote the use of evidence in program and policy development through building the skills, confidence and effectiveness of our local partners so that accessibility and quality of services they provide to communities can be improved. Migunani places emphasis on capacity building as a means to an end; that being the building of resilient and healthier communities. In line with this underpinning philosophy, the Migunani evaluation team will work in close partnership based on mutual need and respect for the skills and expertise that each partner will bring to the programme.

Vision

Legitimate knowledge sector and effective in improving the quality policy

Corporate Strategy

As part of the knowledge sector, Migunani’s role is to produce knowledge through research activities and program evaluation of government policies and the donor institutions

Policy Strategy

Migunani focuses on research and evaluation of donor agencies and government policy (evaluating policy) on the areas of policy agenda (agenda setting) and policy drafting (legislation) which is conducted in the network with other agencies

Focus Issues

Migunani’s subject of concern is basic education , disaster risk reduction , public health , community development, and gender equity

The Position in Civil Society

Migunani takes a role as knowledge producers , giving feedback to the agency that has expertise on advocacy network

The Values

We are committed to the importance of the evidence (evidence - based) policy making , the importance of providing the best quality (quality service ) , respecting the diversity of Indonesia ( pluralism ) , transformative , and a commitment to maintaining the integrity of both the space domestic and public
